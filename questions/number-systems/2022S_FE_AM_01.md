# 2022S_FE_AM_01

Convert the binary number `1011` to its decimal equivalent.

a) 9
b) 10
c) 11
d) 12
?
c) 11

### Explanation

Expand by place values (powers of 2):

```
1011₂ = 1×2³ + 0×2² + 1×2¹ + 1×2⁰
      = 8 + 0 + 2 + 1
      = 11
```

### Why the Others Won't Fit

**a) 9** — miscounts as `1001`.
**b) 10** — miscounts as `1010`.
**d) 12** — miscounts as `1100`.
