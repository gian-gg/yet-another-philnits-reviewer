# 2022S_FE_AM_52

Which property defines an activity that lies on the **critical path** of a
project schedule?

a) It has the most resources assigned.
b) Its slack (float) is zero.
c) It is always the longest individual task.
d) It can be delayed without impact.
?
b) Its slack (float) is zero.

### Explanation

A **critical-path activity** has **zero float** — its earliest start equals
its latest start. Any delay on such an activity delays the whole project
finish date. The critical path is the longest _path_ through the network,
not the longest single task.

### Why the Others Won't Fit

**a) Most resources**:

- Resource loading is independent of the critical path.

**c) Always the longest individual task**:

- It's the longest **path**, not the longest single task.

**d) Can be delayed without impact**:

- That's non-critical (positive float) activities.
